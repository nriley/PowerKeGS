#### ADB Explorer

**What is it?** A troubleshooting tool that displays information about ADB devices attached to an Apple IIGS.

**How do I install it?** Run it directly from the distribution disk, or drag ADB.Explorer onto another disk. 

**How do I use it?** Open the application.

**How do I uninstall it?** Drag it to the Trash.

**Questions?** Email `pkgs@sabi.net`.
